About Together we can make a difference

Who we are

Our association members are part of the Ashinaga Africa Initiative (AAI), Leadership program; (Ashinaga is an NGO that gives educational and emotional support for the Japanese; and Sub Saharan African scholars to study abroad. Our members consist of scholars and graduates dedicated to bringing a positive change to Africa. Majority of the members are offering various majors in different universities across the globe.

What we do

  • Unite the members through professional and fun activities; including webinars to enhance our leadership, entrepreneurial, networking and professional development.
  • Support the new AAI scholars arriving at their Universities across the globe.
  • Connect members to opportunities across the globe.
  • Provide a convenient communication platform for the members.
  • Engage in fundraising activities (Bokin, ​募金​) for the Ashinaga foundation.
  • Collaborate with Ashinaga in the organisation of international conferences (Tsudoi, 集い)
  • Work on our future dreams (​志​) with a deep focus on the development of Africa. Members projects are very diverse in the fields including; Entrepreneurship, Business, Agriculture, Architecture, Design, Innovation, Engineering, Education, Psychology, Medicine, Health, Environment, IT, Humanities/ liberals arts among others.

History

Since its start (2016) in Japan by the Ashinaga African scholars as a fundraising mobilizing unit for the Ashinaga foundation, AISA has grown in size and scope of activities. As of October 2020, our population approximates 200 with roughly at least 4 scholars from each of the 49 sub-Saharan countries in Africa. We now have a series of activities, members have a platform to generate and develop ideas and projects with a deep commitment to Africa's development.
